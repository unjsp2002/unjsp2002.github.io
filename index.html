About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University (SNU), majoring in Materials Science & Chemistry. My research primarily focuses on the **biochemical mechanisms of neurological disorders**, using **chemical tools to investigate metabolic pathways**.

In particular, my recent research explores **glycogen metabolism in astrocytes and its role in neuropathic pain**.

Research Interests: Neuroscience Biochemistry Psychiatry Metabolomics
